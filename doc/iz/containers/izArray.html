public struct izArray(T)

Parameterized, GC-free array.

izArray(T) implements a single-dimension array of uncollected memory. It internally preallocates the memory to minimize the reallocation fingerprints.

Its layout differs from built-in D's dynamic arrays and they cannot be cast as T[] however, most of the slicing operations are possible.
